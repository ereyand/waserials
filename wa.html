Modus Operandi, M.O

Yates targeted high risk women that majority were sex workers and her would have sex or do drugs with them before killing them. Yates' method of killing was gunshots wounds to the head.

How Technological Advances in Forensic Science Lead to Robert Lee Yates Jr

All of the murders resulted in ballistic evidence and eight of them were done with .25 caliber weapon. Thanks to the ballistic analysis, investigators were able to find out that Yates was originally aiming for the heart and not the head. After the last victim was able to escape, police had DNA evidence that they could test against Yates.
